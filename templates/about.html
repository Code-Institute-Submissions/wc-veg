This website is dedicated to maintaining a database to provide and share information about growing vegetables in West Cork, Ireland. There are many great gardeners in West Cork, following the organic way, growing vegetables, trees, flowers, shrubs, mushrooms and probably more. But this site is focused on vegetables in all their forms. There are a number of different categories (such as leafy, root, bulb) into which vegetables are classified. Links are provided to show or edit the information. This is intended to be a community resource, so that West Cork vegetable gardeners can share tips and tricks for getting the best crops while working in harmony with nature.
